About Me

Hi, my name is Jack Bendicsen. I am 19 years old and am from Wheaton, Illinois. This is a town about 40 minutes from the Chicago and right next to Naperville. I started playing music at the age of 12. Shortly after this, my love for music took off. I started on guitar but throughout highschool played bass guitar and ukulele and am currently learning piano. I played in a few jazz bands in highschool, the highschool showband, and a few different bands with my friends. I used to work at the music store in my town, Suburban Music. Here I would work the cash register, manage inventory and lesson schedulinng, along with fixing all stringed intruments. It was here where I really found my passion of luthier work. I have worked on a variety of instruments including guitars (acoustic, classical, and electric), bass guitars, ukuleles, banjos, mandolins, lap harps, drums, violins, violas, and cellos.
